What is The Build Sheet

The Build Sheet is an Automotive Focused Website To Allow Automotive Enthusiasts a Place Where They Can View Others Builds To Make Adjustments To Their Own Car - Made by Enthusiasts - Home of New Jersey - We Want to Make the Car Community a Better Place

Our Mission

We are here to make life build your dream car an easier place. We want to allow other people to share their builds to allow a more home friendly place for car enthusiasts. Take part in it and become a member of the family.
